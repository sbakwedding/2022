Venue

We're looking forward to seeing you in Wassenaar, where Kirsten grew up.
Castle Oud-Wassenaar is accessible by car, bus and even bicycle if you so desire. If you want to take the bus, you can take bus 43 from Den Haag Centraal to Wassenaar/Leiden; the stop is named Stoeplaan. From there is a 10 minute walk, so bring comfortable shoes. If you are feeling active, you can go by bicycle - it will take you about 26 minutes if you start from Den Haag Centraal.
